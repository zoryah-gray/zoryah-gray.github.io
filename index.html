Bio

My name is Zoryah Gray, and I am a current sophomore at Northwestern University, graduating in June 2025. I am studying Computer Science, and I am a QuestBridge Match Scholarship Recipient from Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

I am currently working as an assistant researcher at Amaral Labs, collaborating with a graduate student. My current research focuses on the extraction of meta information from Microsoft Word documents. Moreover, I am an active participant in a three-person team for a Game Jam within the RTVF 376: Advanced Game Studio class at Northwestern University. Our team's objective is to develop a Unity game centered around the theme of sustainability, as part of the Game Jam class project.

Outside of school, I enjoy graphic design, writing, and reading. I have had the opportunity to apply my graphic design skills by creating two business cards for an aspiring bakery. Additionally, in my role as publicity chair for the Rainbow Alliance, I have designed twenty promotional graphics for various events.
